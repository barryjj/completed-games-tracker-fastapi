SteamGridDB

Credentials

SteamGridDB hosts community-uploaded cover art. The key is used to look up candidate covers when you click "Find cover" on a library entry.

Get yours at steamgriddb.com/profile/preferences/api

How It Works

Once your API key is saved, the More menu on each library entry's detail pane gains options to find covers, hero images, and logos. The search term is pre-filled with the game title but can be edited in the picker.

  • Find vertical cover — 600×900 portrait (library card art).
  • Find horizontal cover — 460×215 landscape (header art).
  • Find hero image — wide banner for the detail pane hero block.
  • Find logo — transparent logo overlay in the detail pane hero. Also fetched automatically when the Steam CDN logo is missing.

For Steam entries, the lookup uses the appid directly. For other sources, it falls back to a title search.

Bulk Fill

Walk your library and apply the top SGDB candidate to every entry missing art of that type. Skips entries that already have Steam CDN art or a custom override. Runs in the background; you'll get a toast when it finishes.

Horizontal covers — 460×215 landscape (header art)
Hero images — wide banner for the detail pane
